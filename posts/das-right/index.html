Das Right

Aug 11, 2019 · Alexander

The word “rights” is the most prominent example of language abuse for politics sake.

  1. It is short and not some yet another pseudo scientific “-ism”, “-fobia”. So it looks more natural on the outset;
  2. It has “right” in it :)
  3. You can create new “rights” every day by adding different perfixes;
  4. You can use it to extract something from somebody;
  5. Since it is vague term it can have broad uses especially when it comes to general public;
  6. It also informs users on how to act/behave when demanding something from others: righteously;
