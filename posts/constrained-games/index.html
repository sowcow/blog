Constrained Games

Alexander · 2019.5.30

Constraints in mobile games are everywhere. And they are so explicit.

I have no idea why people voluntary accept constraints. Maybe because there are “opportunities” available around the constraints.

Somebody has to make a game where number of opportunities is decreacing all the time. To observe the influence.
